Developing Effective Pest and Disease Management Strategies for Rice

Automated Detection of Rice Plant Diseases through Leaf Imagery

Rice is one of the most important crops in the world, feeding billions of people. However, rice plants can get sick from various diseases, which can greatly reduce how much rice is harvested. Traditionally, farmers and experts have had to inspect rice plants by hand to spot these diseases, but this method can be slow, tiring, and sometimes inaccurate, especially when dealing with large rice fields.

What:- Leveraging computer vision technology to develop automated systems for detecting diseases in rice plants through the analysis of leaf imagery.

Where:- In rice farming regions, particularly in areas where disease outbreaks can significantly impact crop productivity and farmer livelihoods.

Who:- Farmers, agricultural researchers, technology developers, and agricultural extension services focused on enhancing crop management and disease prevention in rice cultivation.
