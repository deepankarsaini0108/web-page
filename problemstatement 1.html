Farmer Sentiment Analysis on Pest and Disease Management Techniques

Effective pest and disease management is crucial for sustaining agricultural productivity and ensuring food security. However, the success of these management techniques relies not only on their scientific validity but also on their acceptance and adoption by farmers.

What:- Analyze farmers' sentiments regarding pest and disease management techniques using NLP (Natural Language Processing).

Where:- In agricultural communities and regions where pest and disease management is a critical concern, particularly in areas heavily reliant on farming for livelihood.

Who:- Farmers and agricultural stakeholders.

Why:- Understanding the attitudes and concerns of farmers toward these techniques is crucial for improving adoption rates and effectiveness. By analyzing sentiment, we can identify pain points, preferences, and the effectiveness of different methods.
